How To Use This Journal
One signal = one trade = 4 TPs. You open 4 positions from one setup. This journal logs all 4 TPs inside one entry — so 15 trades per day = 15 entries, not 60.
1
Before the trade — identify your setup
Check 15M for direction, 5M for confirmation, 1M for your Order Block. When a candle closes inside the OB box on the 1M chart — that's your signal.
2
Open the trade on MT5
Place 4 positions at the same entry. Each one has its own TP: TP1 (instant/2min), TP2 (5-10min), TP3 (10-15min), TP4 (runner).
3
Log the trade HERE
Tap "Log Trade." Pick direction, session, enter entry price and SL. As each TP closes, tap HIT or MISS and enter the pips. Save when done.
4
Watch the Daily Risk Monitor
The dashboard tracks your daily loss and consecutive losses automatically. When you see the red STOP banner — close MT5. No exceptions.
5
Press "New Day" each morning
This resets the daily counter. Your history is always saved — only the daily stats reset.
🛑 Daily Stop Rules
🔴3 losses in a row → Close MT5. Done for today. No exceptions.
🔴$30 total daily loss → Stop immediately. Come back tomorrow.
🟡2 losses, 1 win, 2 losses → Market is choppy. Stop.
🟢First 3 trades positive → Market is clean. Continue.
